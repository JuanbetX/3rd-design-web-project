History and context

Premise

James Sunderland arrives in Silent Hill after receiving a letter seemingly written by his deceased wife. The fog-shrouded town reveals the guilt and inner desires of those who visit it..

Key characters

  • James Sunderland: Protagonist searching for answers about his past.
  • María: A figure that reflects James's desires and contradictions.
  • Ángela y Eddie: Presences with their own conflicts, mirrors of human pain.

Symbolism

The fog, the silence, and certain creatures function as metaphors for guilt, denial, and punishment, reinforcing the psychological themes of the game.
